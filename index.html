🚌 Charter Operators (Call These)

Recommended call order. Ask each: "Friday July 24, 2026 afternoon, 5-hour block, 25-passenger mini-coach, downtown Hyatt Regency pickup, 3 brewery stops."

▶ Budget reality check

Indy mini-coach charter rates run roughly $125-175/hr. 5 hours = $625-$875 vehicle. Plus 18-22% gratuity + fuel surcharge → expect $900-$1,200 all-in. With 25 brothers that's $36-48/person — well under the $75/person budget. Buffer covers tip + any surprises. Book by mid-May 2026: Indy summer Fridays book 8-10 weeks ahead.
